# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

ORA Navigator is a project supporting Morgan State University's Office of Research Administration. We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone interacting with the project.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Focus on what is best for the research-administration community
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Publishing others' private information (user data, credentials)
- Deliberately submitting misleading information to the knowledge base
- Attempting to exploit the chatbot to access other users' data

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **ask.ora@morgan.edu**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated by the Office of Research Administration.
